Fab Lab Limerick started in 2012 when we began to build our own 3D printers, CNC Routers and laser cutters as part of the elective programme at the School of Architecture, University of Limerick.

We are a community of people passionate about digital fabrication technologies and their potential in creative projects. You are welcome to join us!
